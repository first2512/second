Loving you in silence

This night is quiet
Only the clock ticking in my room
I wrote this poem while imagining
you

I don’t know much about you
Yet I am already falling in love with you
It’s difficult to understand
Yet I like

Without meeting, only communicating
via intermediary

But my heart has fallen to you
A girl who is 4 years younger than
Me

I used to not believe that
love is blind
Now, I believe it
After meeting you

Why can I love you this much?
I count back how happy
I would be if I could be with you
Although it’s difficult
I'm happy to feel in love
Again

Loving you in silence
is my favorite currently
Quiet but not disturbing

Sometimes longing comes
What can I do?
Sometimes, I feel confused
Yet I realize
that love doesn’t always go where you want it to go
So let me like this
Loving you in silence.
